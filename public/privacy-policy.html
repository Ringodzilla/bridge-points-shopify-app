BridgePoint Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-16

BridgePoint is a lightweight Shopify app that helps merchants manage store-credit-based point operations. This policy explains what data the app processes, why it processes that data, and how the data is protected.

1. Data we process

  • Shop domain and app installation metadata
  • Customer identifiers required to display balances and apply store credit
  • Customer name and email when needed for merchant-facing admin operations
  • Order identifiers and order totals needed to process order-paid automatic grants
  • Store credit transaction data returned by Shopify
  • BridgePoint settings, manual grant logs, and idempotency keys

2. Why we process data

  • Display customer store credit balances and recent history
  • Allow merchants to manually grant store credit from Shopify admin
  • Generate and support automated grant workflows
  • Prevent duplicate grant execution
  • Provide merchant-facing dashboards and operational logs

3. Data minimization

BridgePoint uses Shopify Store Credit as the source of truth for balances. The app stores only the minimum supplementary data required for settings, logs, and duplicate-execution prevention. The app does not maintain a separate customer balance ledger as its source of truth.

4. Sharing and selling data

BridgePoint does not sell personal data. Data is processed only to provide the merchant features described above and is not used for unrelated advertising or profiling purposes.

5. Retention

Operational data is retained only as long as needed for app functionality, merchant support, compliance, and troubleshooting. Merchants can request deletion as part of app uninstall and Shopify privacy compliance workflows.

6. Security

  • Data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS in production
  • Access to merchant data is limited to authorized operational use
  • Only the minimum required customer fields are requested

7. Contact

For privacy or data protection questions, contact the BridgePoint operator through the support contact provided in the Shopify App Store listing or merchant onboarding materials.

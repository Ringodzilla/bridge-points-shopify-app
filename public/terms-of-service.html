BridgePoint Terms of Service

Last updated: 2026-05-16

These terms govern the merchant use of BridgePoint, a Shopify app that provides a lightweight points foundation using Shopify Store Credit as the balance source of truth.

1. Service scope

BridgePoint provides merchant-facing tools for settings management, manual store credit grants, customer balance visibility, lightweight KPI dashboards, and workflow support for automated grants.

2. Merchant responsibilities

  • Maintain valid Shopify permissions and account access required to operate the app
  • Review point rules, expiry rules, and workflow configurations before production use
  • Verify automated grant workflows in an appropriate test environment before rollout

3. Billing

Paid plans, if enabled, are billed through Shopify’s supported billing systems. Pricing, plan scope, and billing timing are described in the app’s Shopify listing and plan selection UI.

4. Availability and changes

The service may change over time to improve safety, compatibility, and merchant experience. Features in beta or under evaluation may be updated, limited, or removed.

5. Limitation of liability

BridgePoint is provided on an as-available basis. Merchants remain responsible for their operational policies, campaign rules, and final financial decisions related to store credit and customer support.

6. Contact

Support and contract contact information should be provided in the merchant-facing app listing or onboarding materials used in production.
